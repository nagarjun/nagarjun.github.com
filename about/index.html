Hello!

I'm Nagarjun but you can call me Nag. I'm the Founder & CEO at Eduora, a mobile learning company that I founded while I was still in college. I'm also a full-stack web developer & designer.

I absolutely enjoy creating great products built around solid user-interfaces. My work is mostly web-based however, I've had the chance to craft mobile applications more recently for Eduora.

I built an interest for startups & entrepreneurship at a very young age. My first company was a services business that I started back in the 9th grade after winning a global Microsoft competition. I won a much-needed HP server that also caught the attention of the press after I had secretly participated in my father's name as I was under-age. I also ran one of India's largest developer group (alongside Janakiram M) after high-school & participated in (and won) a few more competitions.

I've always wanted to build a products-based company (a scarcity in India) & I realized this dream when I started Eduora. Much credit goes to Abhishek Rungta and Pallav Nadhani for getting me started.

Among other things, I'm deeply interested in the future of technology, wearable computing, aviation, space, public speaking, travel & urban-planning. You may also find me glued to CNN or BBC if I have the TV on.

A Quick Note About Eduora

Eduora is building the world's first mobile-learning platform for higher-education. More than 70% students are carrying their smartphones to class once they reach high-school & this is set to grow further. The Eduora mobile app gathers highly granular data about students by using a feedback loop built into the remaining collaborative functions. It's the easiest way to get a highly-functional & branded mobile app for your campus.

Eduora was also the first international startup to graduate from The Alchemist Accelerator in Silicon Valley. Learn more here.
